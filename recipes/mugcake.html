Mug Cake

A quick little microwave dessert that you'll love!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa Powder - 1/4 cups
  • Flour - 1/2 cups
  • Milk - 3 Tbsp
  • Sugar - 1/3 cups
  • Neutral Oil - 2 Tbsp

Directions

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a microwave-friendly cup
  2. Add in the wet ingredients and mix well
  3. microwave the cup for around 2 minutes ... and there you go!
