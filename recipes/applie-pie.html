Apple Pie

Indulge in this crispy and creamy apple pie, with a slight tinge of cinnamon!

Ingredients

  • Flour - 1 cup
  • egg - 1
  • cinnamon - 2 tbsp
  • unsalted butter - 2 cups
  • apples - 2 to 3

Directions

  1. Make the dough using the flour and eggs
  2. Make the make the filling using apples, butter and cinnamon
  3. Put the filling into the pie pan and decorate it however you want
  4. Put it in the oven and wait hehe
