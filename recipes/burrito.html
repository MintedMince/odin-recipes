Burrito

The right meal to satisfy a mid week lunch craving in no time!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla - 1
  • Chicken thigh - 1
  • Your faourite sauces
  • Panko bread crumbs - as required

Directions

  1. Cut the thighs into peices and them salt and bread them
  2. Deep fry the thighs till they are golden brown
  3. Make whatever sauce you want lol
  4. Heat the tortilla to make them nice and toasty
  5. Put everything together and there you go!
